Cover RATIONALE

In a time marked by increasing global division and diversity, YSD stands resolute in championing the messages of inclusivity and sustainability. To stand shoulder to shoulder, embracing our unique differences, is to witness empowerment in our collective form, truly emphasising ‘All for All’. Much like clay inspiring endless possibilities, YSD is committed to moulding a better future, working alongside the three Sime Darby Companies and our project partners, as we continue to remind ourselves that our opposites are not contradictory; they’re complementary.

ABOUT YAYASAN SIME DARBY

Yayasan Sime Darby (YSD) is the philanthropic arm of Sime Darby Berhad, Sime Darby Plantation Berhad and Sime Darby Property Berhad. Over the years, YSD has expanded its wings from offering scholarships to outstanding and deserving individuals to funding impactful conservation, outreach and development programmes.

Our Vision

To lead and make a sustainable impact and a difference in the lives of others.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to enhancing lives and delivering sustainable value through the following five pillars:

Education

Environment

Sports

We practise high ethical values and observe good corporate governance.
